The first round is the written test, consisting of quantitative aptitude, reasoning (logical ability), English (verbal ability) and computer science subjects (C, C++, Data Structures, Algorithms, DBMS, OS, Networks, etc.). For some companies, you might also have a coding round, which is sometimes taken as a separate round and sometimes is clubbed with the written test. Further rounds are interview rounds, the first two usually being the technical round and successive rounds being the HR round.
